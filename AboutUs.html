About Fox Location Services

Local knowledge. Personal commitment.

A trusted bridge between international productions and the people, places and practical detail of central Italy.

Meet the founder

A passion for place, backed by experience.

My name is Jeni Fox, founder of Fox Location Services. Originally from the UK, I have been living in Italy for the past eight years and have become completely captivated by its extraordinary landscapes, rich history, and hidden treasures. After years of simply enjoying and exploring this beautiful part of the world, I decided to combine my passion for the region with my professional experience as a project manager to help showcase this remarkable area to a wider audience.

My mission is to share this true diamond of a place with the world, revealing the beauty, authenticity, and diversity of what is known as the Green Heart of Italy. Over the years, I have developed extensive local knowledge and built strong, trusted relationships throughout the region.

Jeni Fox Founder · Fox Location Services

Connected and capable

The local detail that keeps a production moving.

Through my network of contacts, I can facilitate access to accommodation providers, catering services, equipment and vehicle rentals, and key local authorities and bureaucratic offices. I also work closely with trusted professionals in first aid, security, and civil protection services, ensuring productions receive reliable, professional support at every stage.

As an experienced project manager, I understand the importance of organisation, problem-solving, communication, and attention to detail. My aim is to combine these skills with my local knowledge and connections to provide a seamless, personalised service that helps bring creative visions to life while introducing filmmakers and creatives to one of Italy’s best-kept secrets.

Fox Location Services was born from a simple belief: that the Green Heart of Italy deserves to be discovered by the world.
